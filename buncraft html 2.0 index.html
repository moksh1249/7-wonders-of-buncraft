CONVENANT 1
RESPECT CONVENANT

? Any form of discriminatory comments (racism, sexism, etc.) and foul or inappropriate language is strictly PROHIBITED.

? Any form of harassment towards other players is NOT allowed. This includes trolling.

? Do NOT attack nor kill players without their permission.

? No caps, begging, flooding and spamming.

CONVENANT II
DECEIVER COVENANT

? NO hacks, hack clients or any form of cheating.

? Using x-ray, block/item duplication hacks, fly-speed hacks, mini-maps, bug exploits or anything that will give you an unfair advantage over the rest is NOT allowed.

? If you are unsure about any third-party tool that can potentially be considered cheating, ask a developer.

? Do NOT abuse any bugs or exploits. Report it right away to the Developers

CONVENANT III
BUILD COVENANT

? Do NOT travel and build on the nether roof.

? Building any form of AFK farms is NOT allowed.

? Building FULLY automatic farms is not allowed. Semi-automatic farms are allowed AS LONG AS it operates ONLY when a player interacts with it. If your farm still operates without a player interaction, that’s called a FULLY automatic farm.

? Building lag-inducing contraptions such as too much redstone and entity-heavy machines are NOT allowed.

? NO inappropriate builds

? Do NOT alter the landscape (wilderness) if you do NOT intend to build on it.

? Certain grinders/mob farms are strictly PROHIBITED to reduce lag and balance the economy, namely:
pigman/wither/irongolem/enderman/wither/ghast

? Do NOT touch or otherwise take anything that is not yours (AKA griefing and/or stealing). This rule includes:

TERRITORY COVENANT
